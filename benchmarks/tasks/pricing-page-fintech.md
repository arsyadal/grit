# Task: Fintech Pricing Page

## Product Context

The product is a digital investment platform with retail and advisor tiers.

## User Goal

Explain pricing clearly, reduce confusion, and drive plan selection.

## Deliverable

Generate a responsive pricing page.

## Requirements

- pricing tier comparison
- feature differentiation
- FAQ or reassurance section
- clear CTA

## Tech Stack

React + Tailwind CSS

## Constraints

- should feel premium and high-trust
- avoid cliché SaaS pricing cards
- typography should carry persuasion
